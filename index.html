About us...

Located in the beautiful city of York, Koodi is a friendly, family run team. All our clients are important to us, whatever industry or size your company is. We feel lucky that we get to work with such fantastic customers, creating bespoke branding and websites to showcase their company.

Every company/client has their individual requirements, aims and timescales, which is why all our quotes are tailored to each project and its specific needs as opposed to a “one package fits all” scenario.

All our websites are designed to be fully responsive (mobile friendly), allowing your site to adapt to different screen sizes, so you can provide visitors with the best possible experience.
